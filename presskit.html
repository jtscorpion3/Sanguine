PITCH

Get your blasters ready for some high octane action and adventure across this top-down single player shooter. The self-proclaimed Totally Overpowered Machine is trying to take over the world and he's got his aim locked in on YOU. Play as Damon, the cocky cadet, ready to take him down once and for all. Choose from a variety of over 70+ skills and traverse across 40 corrupted levels catered to nostalgic video game fans from around the world.

DEFEAT THE VIRUS, DISCOVER YOUR PLAYSTYLE

Pete the Garage Guy knows his craft, and that's building you upgrades you'll need to defeat T.O.M. Choose to upgrade: basic stats, a new weapon, and powerful active and passive abilites! In Rogue Mode, T.O.M hacked Pete's upgrading system. Choose from abilities you recognize from the story mode that have now been corrupted back to their original prototype versions. Choose wisely or you'll find yourself back at square one.

STORY

The story takes place inside of the Origin Sectors, a generated world inside of humanities central server system, with each sector acting as its own motherboard. T.O.M, the main antagonist, wishes to gain control of each sector in an attempt to make a himself a supreme admin across all servers in the world. Sector VIII is the last surviving sector and the smallest of all 11 sectors. Damon, the protagonist, is the last hope of saving the sectors and restoring the Origin Sectors to what it once was. You must stop T.O.M before he gains world domination.

SKILL TREE

Let's cut to the chase, you need powerful weapons to take down this infesting Virus, choose from abilities like Time Capsule an area of effect zone that slows enemies and damages over time. Maneuver your way through levels with Wave Dash, a repositioning dash ability. Liking Wave Dash? Upgrade it to afflict damage on each enemy you travel through. Blast enemies away with Pulse Blast and cause them to spin out with Nano-Tornado. Is straight-up damage your forte? Try using Engine Burst to deal massive damage in a huge explosion around the XV9 ship. Want to lower your cooldowns? We got skills for that too!

GAMEMODES

In addition to the Story, we got 3 other jampacked modes ready to test your skills. GAUNTLET - Try out new skill combinations across these high-octane arenas. SURVIVAL - Defeat infinite waves of viral attacks. Buy powerups and unlock new areas and ROGUE - T.O.M hacked his way into the installation system, skill choices and levels have been randomized.

NIGHTMARE

T.O.M's tough, but he's even tougher when we crank the difficulty meter to 1000%. Try defeating T.O.M in our toughest challenge. You'll cry, rage, maybe even break a controller.(It's not our fault if it does) ;)
