About the game


GET YOUR BLASTERS READY FOR SOME HIGH OCTANE ACTION AND ADVENTURE

The self-proclaimed Totally Overpowered Machine is trying to take over the world, and he's got his aim locked and ready at Sector VIII.
Play as Damon, the cocky cadet, ready to take him down once and for all. Choose from a variety of over 70+ skills and traverse across 20+ levels corrupted by T.O.M's onslaught virus.

DEFEAT THE VIRUS, DISCOVER YOUR PLAYSTYLE

Pete the Garage Guy knows his craft, and that's building you upgrades you'll need to defeat T.O.M. Choose to upgrade Healing, Shielding, Damage, Critical Strike, Rate of Fire and more!
In Rogue Mode, T.O.M hacked the upgrade system, choose from abilities you recognize from the story mode that have been corrupted back to their original prototype versions. Choose wisely or you'll find yourself back at square one.
