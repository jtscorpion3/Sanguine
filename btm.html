Meet the Characters:

DAMON - THE COCKY CADET

Damon is the main protagonist of Beat the Machine: Rebooted. His cocky and I can do it all attitude might be the help that Sector VIII needs in defending the nasty attacks from T.O.M. While Damon might still be a rookie in the eyes of both Syncopia and Logan, his goal is to save Sector VIII from total destruction.

T.O.M - TOTALLY OVERPOWERED MACHINE

T.O.M. is the Evil villain behind the attacks toward Sector VIII. His virus is running rampant throughout the different layers of the Origin Sectors. With shell, speed, and more viruses he has created, he plans to destroy Syncopia’s mainframe and take over all the Origin Sectors. Sector VIII is the last standing sector and it’s up to Damon to save everyone and everything T.O.M has his eyes on.

SYNCOPIA - LEADER OF SECTOR VIII

Syncopia is the leader and heart of Sector VIII. Once the world’s premier fighter she took a long hiatus and is a bit rusty on field operations. Now powering the motherboard, she needs help on the inside and calls for assistance. She needs someone who isn’t afraid to fight T.O.M head on and defend her home.

PETE - THE GARAGE GUY

Pete is the customizations expert for Sector VIII and knows everything about the XV9 ship. He built all his own upgrades from scratch; the pitfall of these upgrades is that the more powerful the upgrades are, the less corruption there must be to use them. Taking out more of the virus will allow Pete to build more upgrades.

LOGAN - THE ANNOYING BRIEFING SPECIALIST

Logan is the annoying briefing specialist that teaches the ins and outs of the XV9 ship. Once a parcel exporter for Sector V, Logan brings his expertise of location knowledge and knowing how to the drive the ship. Logan teaches new cadets how to drive and defend home base.
